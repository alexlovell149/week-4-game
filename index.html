Gem Miner

You will be given a random number at the start of this game

There are four crystals below here. If you click on a crystal you will add specific amount of points to your score

You win by matching your total score to the random number, you lose if you bust or go over that number

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal
